Runways

Windis the single most important factor that dictates runway deisgn, because a head on wind adds lift reducing the power required for take-off, and reduces landing speed. It also maximises the operational hours of the airport in windy conditions. The alternative is landing with heavy crosswinds, which is not particularly fun for the passengers or easy for the pilot, if the pilot can land at all. If overlay the prevailing winds in their area the pattern becomes clear.
